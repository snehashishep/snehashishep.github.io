About my research

What am I working on?

  • Hunting for unconventional signatures of New Physics at future colliders, especially the Muon Collider.
  • Implementing various Machine Learning techniques to discern weaker signals at the colliders.
  • Analysing the exciting phenomenology of extended scalar sectors.
  • Studying the nature of Dark Matter, one model at a time.

What do I use?

  • Simulation and analysis: Pythia8, MadGraph, Delphes, ROOT.
  • Model building and other tools: CalcHEP, SARAH, SPheno, MicrOMEGAs, HiggsTools, CosmoTransitions.
  • Programming: C/C++, Python, Mathematica, Bash Scripts, LaTeX, HTML/CSS.
